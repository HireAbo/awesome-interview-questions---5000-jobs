# Certified Nurse Midwife Interview Prep

Welcome to the Certified Nurse Midwife Interview Preparation ! This resource is designed to help you ace your Certified Nurse Midwife interviews with confidence.

A Certified Nurse Midwife (CNM) is a healthcare professional who specializes in providing comprehensive healthcare to women throughout their lifespan, particularly during pregnancy, childbirth, and postpartum. CNMs are highly trained registered nurses who have obtained advanced education and certification in midwifery. They are licensed to provide a range of services, including prenatal care, gynecological exams, family planning counseling, labor and delivery assistance, and postpartum care. CNMs work collaboratively with obstetricians and other healthcare providers to ensure the safety and well-being of both mother and baby. They play a crucial role in promoting and supporting natural childbirth while also offering medical interventions when necessary.
